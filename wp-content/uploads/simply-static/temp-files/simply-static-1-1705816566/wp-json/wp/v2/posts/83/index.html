Ingredients<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • 4 tbsp self-raising flour<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder, plus extra for dusting<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 1 medium egg<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 3 tbsp whole milk<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 3 tbsp sunflower oil<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • \u00bc tsp vanilla extract<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 40g dark chocolate, roughly chopped<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • a pinch sea salt flakes<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Method<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    STEP 1<\/strong>
    Measure the self-raising flour, caster sugar and cocoa powder into a large mug, and mix together. Crack in the egg, pour in the milk, oil and vanilla extract and whisk with a fork until no lumps of flour remain and you have a smooth cake batter.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    STEP 2<\/strong>
    Add the dark chocolate and mix briefly again, then the top with sea salt flakes. Put into the middle of your microwave oven and cook on high for 1\u00bd-2 minutes or until firm to the touch. Dust with a little cocoa powder to serve,\u00a0if\u00a0you\u00a0like.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
