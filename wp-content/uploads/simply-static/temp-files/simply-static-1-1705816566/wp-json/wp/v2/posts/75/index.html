Ingredients<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • 2 Little Gem, cut into chunky pieces<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 50g sugar snap peas, halved<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 2 tbsp capers<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 150g hot-smoked salmon<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • crusty bread, to serve (optional)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Method<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

    STEP 1<\/strong>
    To make the dressing, whisk together the buttermilk, vinegar, sugar and some seasoning and stir in the chives.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    STEP 2<\/strong>
    Add the lettuce to a large bowl with the sugar snaps, red onion and capers and toss gently. Pour in 1\/2 the dressing and toss again. Flake over the salmon and gently toss again.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    STEP 3<\/strong>
    Tip into a serving bowl, drizzle over the rest of the dressing and serve with crusty bread,\u00a0if\u00a0you\u00a0like.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
