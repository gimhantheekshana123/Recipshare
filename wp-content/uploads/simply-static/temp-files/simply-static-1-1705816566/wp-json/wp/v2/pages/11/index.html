Recipshare is a website where recipes all around the world can be reviewed, experienced and shared. Users will be able to search through a wide range of recipes from the Western, Eastern and all most every corner of the world and the facility to submit one’s own recipe to be displayed to the public will be provided for the users. Recipshare is an ad-free website where users will not be interrupted by any advertisements. The ease of accessing the recipes, no interruptions from advertisements, user profile creation, submission of users’ unique recipes are the reasons why Recipshare has become the pioneer user-friendly website throughout the internet.<\/h5>\n\n\n\n
